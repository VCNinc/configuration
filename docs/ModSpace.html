ModSpace

Class representing a Space. A mod space is a subset of a modular network. It is specified as a set of moduli and remainders according to the following syntax: A%B,C%D,E%F… Where B,D,F are moduli and A,C,E are corresponding remainders. Moduli must be powers of 2 and smaller than the network modulus. For example, the modspace "0%2" indicates all mods which are even, and "5%8" indicates all mods which have remainder 5 when divided by 8; "0%2,5%8" indicates all mods that are even or have remainder 5 when divided by 8.
